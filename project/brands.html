Foundations: Federer first started his own charitable foundation called the “Roger Federer Foundation” to provide sports and education to children in places that have little or no funding at all. This foundation is a charitable grant making foundation, that supports innovative projects operated by local relief organizations in selected countries throughout the world. Federers foundation currently has funds of $1,243,182. In addition, he has supported many charities such as Elton John AIDS Foundation, Humpty Dumpty Foundation, Make-A-Wish Foundation, and many more. Sponsorships: Federer first signed with Nike footwear and apparel in 1994 and has been wearing their shoes and athletic wear up until their contract expired in 2018. After that, he signed a deal with Uniqlo, which would be who Federers new sponsor on the court. In May 2020, Federer became the first tennis player to reach the top of Forbes list of highest paid athletes in the world and was soon endorsed by Credit Suisse, On, Rolex, Lindt, Sunrise, and Jura Elektroapparte. Brands: Roger Federer partnered with the company On, which is a sneaker company that was created in the Swiss Alps. In November of 2021 Roger became an entrepreneur and has become a close partner to the On team. On July 6, 2020, Federer revealed his first sneaker apart of the company called the “THE ROGER Centre Court 0-Series,” and has helped improve the design of the sneaker ever since.
